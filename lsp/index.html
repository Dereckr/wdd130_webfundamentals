About us

The company was founded in the year 2000 in La Paz, Bolivia. It was created with the purpose to offer services as a Logistics Operator, Consolidation, and deconsolidation of international cargo. It works in exports and imports.
The Mission of the company is to offer a personalized service that permits optimizing the request of the clients through efficient support.
